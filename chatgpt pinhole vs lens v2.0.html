Pinhole vs Lens Camera

Pinhole: tiny hole = sharper but dimmer; big hole = brighter but blurrier. Lens: press “Focus”, then slide the screen to make it sharp/blurry.

Controls

Quick classroom demo: 1) Pinhole: drag hole size big → tiny. 2) Lens: click Focus, then move the screen slightly to make it blurry.
