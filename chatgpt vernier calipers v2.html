How to read (quick reminder):
Calipers: main scale gives whole millimetres; add the vernier division that lines up.
Micrometer (0.01 mm): sleeve gives mm + 0.5 mm; thimble gives hundredths (0.01 mm).
Vernier micrometer (0.001 mm): sleeve (mm + 0.5), then thimble (0.01), then vernier (0.001) by the aligned line.
