Space Environment Tennis Visualiser

Compare Earth against a custom environment for four preset tennis shots in a simplified 2D model. The flight includes gravity, drag, Magnus force from spin, and bounce behaviour from ball and surface restitution. The receiver stays fixed where you place them so the key readout is simply the height at which the ball passes.
