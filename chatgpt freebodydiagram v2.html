Quiz

Pick a scenario, press Start, then add forces and labels. When you Submit, you’ll get a multi‑category score and feedback.
Scoring categories: identification (right forces, no extras), labels, directions, and placement/neatness. Direction tolerance is generous; labels accept common equivalents (e.g., W/mg, N/R).
