Design rules cheat-sheet

These are the qualitative “ops card” points, restated:
  • aC ∝ ω v: speed limits matter (carts, bikes, drones).
  • Prefer tangential corridors; radial travel needs rails and gentle lift/stair profiles.
  • Put high-speed activity near the rim; minimise routine radial commuting.
  • Where free jets exist (showers, hoses), keep fall distances short; use enclosures, sprays, angled drains.
  • Precision work and VR/medical tasks: keep ω low; “eyes first, slow head” helps.
  • Avoid routine spin-rate changes (Euler forces) during normal operations.
