Design rules cheat-sheet

  • aC ∝ ω v: speed limits matter (bikes/carts/drones).
  • Prefer tangential corridors; radial travel needs rails and gentle lift/stair profiles.
  • Put high-speed activity near the rim; minimise routine radial commuting.
  • Where free jets exist (showers, hoses), keep fall distances short; use sprays/enclosures and angled drains.
  • Precision/VR/medical work: keep ω low; “eyes first, slow head”.
